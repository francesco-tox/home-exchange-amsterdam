Amsterdam Canal-Side Attic Apartment

Welcome

This characterful attic apartment in central Amsterdam is a warm, curated home perfect for a meaningful home exchange experience. Located near the Jordaan, with views over the canals, it combines modern comfort with authentic Amsterdam charm. The apartment features elegant, gallery-style interiors with natural light, dark parquet floors, and carefully curated details. Ideal for one person or a couple seeking an authentic Amsterdam base with all the modern conveniences.

The building offers elevator access, making it easily accessible. The separate bedroom, open living/dining/kitchen area, and private terrace create a perfect balance of intimacy and social space. Whether you're exploring Amsterdam's museums, cycling through the neighbourhoods, or simply enjoying the canal-side atmosphere from your terrace, this is your home away from home.
