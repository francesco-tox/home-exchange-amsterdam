The person behind the home

I'm Francesco, an Italian based in Amsterdam, where I work in marketing in the tech sector. Before that, I trained as a classical musician, and art, music, books are still a big part of my life. My apartment, purchased in 2025, is my home and I am putting effort into making it feel warm, bright and personal. As a guest, I'm quiet, respectful and very careful with other people's homes. I'm interested in home exchanges that feel thoughtful and human: a way to experience a place through someone's real home, with mutual trust and care.
