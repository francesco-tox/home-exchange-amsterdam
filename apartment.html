The Apartment

A charming attic with views and privacy.

A top-floor Amsterdam apartment with beams, sloped ceilings, a separate bedroom, a full bathroom, a small private terrace and views over water, rooftops and green.

Canals, rooftops and green.

The surrounding views are part of the atmosphere: water, rooflines, trees and the changing light around the Jordaan edge.

01 Living & Kitchen

The main room.

The living and kitchen area is open, bright and practical, with roof windows, visible beams and enough space to cook, eat, read or spend a quiet evening at home.

02 Bedroom

A quiet retreat.

A separate bedroom away from the main room: compact, calm and private, with the softer feeling of the attic architecture.

03 Bathroom & Separate WC

Bath, shower and separate WC.

A practical full bathroom with both a bathtub and a shower, plus a separate WC that adds useful privacy when hosting.

04 Terrace

A private outside corner.

A small terrace for coffee, plants and evening drinks: an extra breathing space above the city.

A clear layout.
