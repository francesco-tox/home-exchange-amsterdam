The Neighborhood

The Jordaan,
just outside.

The apartment sits on the western edge of the Jordaan, overlooking the Marnixplantsoen and the Lijnbansgracht. Canals, markets and green space are all within a short walk — and transport is right at the door.

On foot from your door

  • 3 minInto the heart of the Jordaan — cafés, galleries and narrow streets
  • 8 minThe canal ring (Prinsengracht) and Westerkerk
  • 10 minWesterpark & the Westergasfabriek — green space and weekend life
  • 10 minHaarlemmerdijk & Haarlemmerstraat — independent shops and food
  • 12 minNoordermarkt — Saturday organic & Monday flea markets
  • 20 minDam Square and the historic centre

Four stops from Centraal

Public transport is at the door, so you can leave the bikes to the locals if you'd rather.

  • ·Bus lines 13, 18 & 21 stop directly in front of the entrance
  • ·Tram 5 and bus 22 a very short walk away
  • 4stops from Amsterdam Centraal Station
  • 20minutes on foot to Dam Square

Markets & green

Weekends revolve around the Jordaan's markets and the open lawns of Westerpark. The Marnixplantsoen right outside is a quiet pocket of green to start the day.

Day to day

Supermarkets, bakeries and pharmacies are all within a few minutes' walk. It's a genuine residential neighbourhood, not a tourist strip.
