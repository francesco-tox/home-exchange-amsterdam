Types of Places I'm Interested In

I'm open to home exchanges in characterful destinations across Europe and beyond. I'm particularly drawn to places with:

🏛️ Culture & Architecture

Historic city centers, authentic neighborhoods, and places with strong local character. I love exploring architecture, galleries, and cultural venues.

🍽️ Food & Local Experiences

Neighborhoods with good restaurants, local markets, and culinary traditions. I enjoy cooking and experiencing regional cuisines.

🌳 Nature & Outdoors

Proximity to parks, countryside, mountains, or coastal areas. Ideal for hiking, cycling, and outdoor exploration.

🚴 Walkability & Transport

Places where cycling and public transportation are the norm. Car-free or car-light neighborhoods are especially appealing.

Whether it's a small historic town, a vibrant city neighborhood, or a countryside cottage, I'm interested in places where I can authentically experience a different way of living.
