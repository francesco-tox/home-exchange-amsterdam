Exchange Preferences

What I'm looking for

I'm open to home exchanges in characterful destinations across Europe and beyond. I'm particularly drawn to cities and regions with:

Rich culture & architecture: Historic city centers, authentic neighborhoods, and places with strong local character.
Food & local experiences: Neighborhoods with good restaurants, markets, and culinary traditions.
Nature & outdoor access: Proximity to parks, countryside, mountains, or coastal areas.
Walkability & public transport: Places where cycling and public transportation are the norm.
Community feel: Neighborhoods where you can experience everyday life like a local, not as a tourist.

Whether it's a small historic town, a vibrant city neighborhood, or a countryside cottage, I'm interested in places where I can authentically experience a different way of living.
