The Exchange

A simple,
honest swap.

A home exchange means I stay in your place while you stay in ours — no money changes hands, just two homes looked after by people who actually live in them.

How it works

01

I get in touch

Tell us a little about you, your home, and the dates you have in mind. I'll do the same — openly and without pressure.

02

I agree the details

Dates, length of stay, who's coming, and any house notes — plants, recycling, the quirks of an old building. Everything written down so I're both comfortable.

03

I swap keys

You make yourself at home in Amsterdam; I do the same with you. I treat each other's homes as I'd want our own treated.
