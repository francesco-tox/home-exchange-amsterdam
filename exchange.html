Exchange Preferences

What we're looking for

Types of Places

We're especially drawn to towns, smaller cities, and villages in Northern Europe — places with character, green surroundings, and walkable centers. For this exchange we're focusing on inland and northern destinations rather than coastal or beach locations.

What Matters to Us

  • Character & local identity: Places where the neighborhood has a distinct feel and reflects local life
  • Walkability: We prefer to walk or bike for daily life rather than rely on cars
  • Green surroundings: Parks, forests, canals, or countryside nearby
  • Cultural venues: Good local restaurants, markets, galleries, and independent shops
  • Transport: Good public transport or compact enough to navigate by foot

Seasonal Preferences

We are flexible on season, though we prefer autumn, winter, and early spring (September–April). We avoid peak summer heat if possible.

Duration

We are open to exchanges of 2–4 weeks, depending on timing and circumstance.
